# Glossary

Definitions used across the dashboard and reports. #reference

- **Net Sales** — revenue after returns and sales deductions.
- **COGS** — Cost of Goods Sold.
- **Gross Margin** — Net Sales minus COGS.
- **Operating Expense (Opex)** — running costs below gross margin.
- **Operating Profit** — Gross Margin minus Operating Expense.
- **Net Income** — profit after tax.
- **Outlook** — the forecast full-year figure; see [[conventions]].
